Phase 1

Required Documents

  • Valid ID
  • Proof of Address
  • Bank Statement
  • Proof of Income
  • Passport size photo

Phase 2

Geo Survey

A survey is done to detect geo points and water depths, soil type and rock formation.

Phase 3

Application

A tailored quote is provided based on the survey results and customer requirements. This application is attached with the required documents and handed over for vetting.

Phase 4

Approval

Once the application is approved, a contract is signed and a deposit is paid. Drilling and installation is scheduled.

Note:Project timeline is 7-21 working days
